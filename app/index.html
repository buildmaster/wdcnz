    Speakers

    Sara Chipps

    @sarajchipps

    A JavaScript developer based in NYC and CTO of Flatiron School, an online school for passionate people. In her free time she works on wearables for http://kittiebots.com and hacks on Nodebots.

    Vanessa Hurst

    @DBNess

    Founder/CEO of CodeMontage, where coders engage in open source projects to improve their skills and the world. Founded Developers for Good and co-founded WriteSpeakCode and Girl Develop It.

    Jen Myers

    @antiheroine

    Web designer/developer and part of the instructor team at Dev Bootcamp in Chicago. Speaks regularly about design, development and diversity, and focuses on finding new ways to make both technology and technology education accessible to everyone.

    James Halliday

    @substack

    Co-founded browserling and testling. Writes on http://substack.net/ by way of unspecified machinations and there are some e-packages on https://github.com/substack and https://www.npmjs.org/~substack that you can use to build your internet commerce website.

    Katie Miller

    @codemiller

    OpenShift Developer Advocate at Red Hat, trying to squeeze in as many different programming languages as possible. Co-Founder of the Lambda Ladies group for women in functional programming and an organiser of the Brisbane Functional Programming Group

    Mark Dalgleish

    @markdalgleish

    Senior developer at SEEK in Melbourne, Australia and the lead organiser of MelbJS. Obsessed with everything web and loves using JavaScript, CSS and HTML to create rich experiences that resonate with end users.

    Silvia Pfeiffer

    @silviapfeiffer

    Open video technology enthusiast with a long background in open source software, media technology R&D, and in open standards. Author of “The Definitive Guide to HTML5 Video”. Now working for NICTA on new applications using WebRTC.

    Colin Bowern

    @colinbowern

    Principal Consultant at ObjectSharp. Having escaped the Canadian winters he is now based in Auckland mentoring software teams on user-centric design and craftmanship.

    Sarah Hui

    @sehsarah

    shui.js – an elegant, lightweight framework for all your JavaScript needs, powering cutting edge cloud-based point of sale at Vend in Auckland.

    Kevin Isom

    @kev_nz

    Development manager at Terabyte Interactive. A front end champion on dev teams, when not writing code he's at local developer community events expressing his opinion or helping other developers

    Toni Barrett

    @toni_b

    Owner of Beauty Bliss - an online store based in Wellington. Originally a side project, it's now Toni's full time job to do everything from product development to the code base. Previously a front end developer at StarNow and BBC Worldwide.
